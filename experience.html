My Experience

Career

I have a decades worth of experience working for companies that build digital products, starting in a project coordinator role for a large retailer just beginning their e-commerce journey back in 2009. Over that time I picked up new and interesting ways of working from colleagues, clients and training I sought out myself. I love to try new things, and to encourage others around me to keep experimenting also with the way that they do things.

My background as a Project Manager has put me in a good position to juggle my time, and it allows me to prioritise learning whilst still being focussed on my day job.
